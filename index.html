About me

I am a PhD student in stem cell biology from the University of Hong Kong & Centre for Translational Stem Cell Biology.

Currently, I am working in Dr Rio Sugimura's lab studying spatiotemporal analysis of early hematopoiesis in human multilineage organoids. I am also working with Dr Yuanhua Huang on single-cell computational analysis of CRISPR/Cas9-based cellular lineage tracing system.
